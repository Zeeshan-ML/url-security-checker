How It Works

1.   Enter a URL or Domain

Type the website URL or domain (e.g. www.example.com or example.com) you want to check

2.   Blacklist Check

We quickly check if the site is on known harmful or safe sites

3.   Analyze the URL

We look for any unusual patterns or suspicious parts of the URL or domain

4.   Behavioral Check

We check for things like missing security (HTTPS) or suspicious redirects that could indicate a dangerous site

5.   Machine Learning Validation

Our system compares the URL with patterns of known phishing sites for extra accuracy

6.   Get Your Result

You will get a simple result: Safe or Dangerous. If unsafe, we explain why and give you tips for staying safe
